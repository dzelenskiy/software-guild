Tasty Tates Grill serves delicious American cuisine with a side of world renowned mashed, fried, or thinly sliced potato sides. We only use localy sourced organic ingredients. All of our dishes start on site from scratch. We make our own italian sausage and hot dogs right here in the kitchen. We make our own cheese and bake fresh bread everyday. Although we offer world class eats, the atmosphere here is a low key neighborhood vibe. We have local musicians performing every Friday and Saturday night, so come on down and join us for a bite of Tates!
